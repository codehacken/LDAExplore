What is this?

A multidimensional explorer of Topic Models results of LDA..

The parallel coordinates displays each topic discovered by the output of the corpus of ducments pass into LDA.

Controls

Brush: Drag vertically along an axis.
Remove Brush: Tap the axis background.
Reorder Axes: Drag a label horizontally.
Invert Axis: Tap an axis label.
Remove Axis: Drag axis label to the left edge.
